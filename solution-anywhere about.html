About US


Solution Anywhere is an open source software development organization headquartered in Chicago, USA. Since our founding, we have been committed to building reliable, scalable, and accessible software solutions that are freely available to developers and organizations around the world.

Our mission is to make quality software accessible to everyone, regardless of budget or location. We believe that open collaboration drives innovation faster than closed, proprietary development, and our projects reflect this philosophy at every stage.

The team at Solution Anywhere consists of experienced software engineers, designers, and contributors from across the globe, all working together to build tools that solve real-world problems. We focus on enterprise application development, persistent data management solutions, and developer tools that simplify complex programming tasks.
Some of the key values that guide our work include:

  • Transparency in how our software is built and maintained

  • Community-driven development and open contribution

  • Continuous improvement through user feedback

  • Commitment to reliability and long-term support

Over the years, Solution Anywhere has grown from a small group of passionate developers into a globally recognized name in open source enterprise solutions. We invite you to explore our projects, contribute to our codebase, and join our growing community of developers.
